MPooh3 Personal Music Player

MPooh3 evolved out of an intensive 10-week introductory course in electrical engineering and hardware design. The only instruction for the final project was to build a MP3 player. Many different types of players flourished, from home-theater sound systems to art installations. I decided to pursue my interest in children development and interaction and build a toddler-specific MP3 player.
