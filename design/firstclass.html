First Class: Interactive Airport Window

First Class is my final project for the Learning, Design, and Technology Master's program at Stanford University. Inspired by many hours spent bored while waiting inside airports, First Class enables travelers to learn about the engineering and cultural marvels in their immediate environment.

By building a functional proof of concept prototype, I demonstrated how time held captive in airports can transform into rich learning experiences.
